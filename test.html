Spectrum War is a quiz competition organized by IEEE LINK based on Spectrum Magazine. The competition is open for all the student branches under the Kerala Section. All IEEE members are welcome to participate in the quiz. The quiz will be based on the latest issue of Spectrum Magazine. The competition provides the participants with a chance to know how much one has perceived from the last edition of Spectrum Magazine.

We hope that you have read and understood the magazines well before you attempt the quiz. The syllabus of the quiz is also provided.
